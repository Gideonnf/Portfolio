## Technical Overview
**Hawker Rush** is a high-speed point-and-click culinary simulation game created in under 6 days for the Singapore Heritage Game Jam 2020. Players assume the role of various street-food vendors scattered across historical locales in Singapore, rapidly preparing authentic regional recipes to fulfill incoming customer queues. 

The software contains two functional loops:
- **Endless Mode:** An unyielding arcade progression scale tracking speed and precision.
- **Story Mode:** A curated sequence guiding users through localized narrative milestones.

## About the Project
Assembling a unified design concept inside a 5-6 day production framework presented immediate challenges under the jam's heritage theme. Our 5-man team prioritized Singapore’s culinary ecosystem due to its historical legacy. Our mechanic choices drew heavy structural influence from classic flash titles such as the old *Bush Dog Game*.

Time box constraints became our primary bottleneck. We originally mapped out sprawling unique levels reflecting multiple distinct ethnic cultures, but ultimately condensed production to three fully realized, highly polished zones to protect baseline stability. Additionally, complex menu trees and long recipes meant players needed robust, onboarding instructions; due to clock limitations, we had to cut down on extended interactive tutorial maps and pivot to lightweight, compact UI systems instead.
