## Technical Overview
**Survival of the Animents** is an arcade-style spell-slinging survival game. It was developed within Unity using C# by a core team consisting of 4 programmers and 4 artists. 

On this project, I engineered multiple gameplay features and UX flows, including:
- **Character Selection & Customization Systems**
- **Player Status Pipelines** (Buffs and debuffs architecture)
- **Enemy Debuff Implementations**
- **Scene Management & Game State Transitions**

Additionally, I managed the art integration for importing asset packages, rigging mechanics, and matching animations to gameplay event timelines.

## About the Project
This game was built as part of Studio Project 4 during my second academic year. This milestone marked my first major multidisciplinary project interfacing closely with a large art team (having previously only worked in micro-teams of two). 

Navigating the cross-talk between developers and artists introduced technical and creative alignment hurdles. I served as a central technical communicator, structuring the programming lifecycle while defining clear constraints for asset pipelines to mitigate integration bottlenecks. Through iterative pipeline refinement, our team met milestones to deploy a cohesive, fully functioning build.
